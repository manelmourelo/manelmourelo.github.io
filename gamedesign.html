Jericó World Building

World Building document made for the Game Design II subject.

Summary:
We live in a world where all humans are born with a mark that relate them to a specific god, we call this mark Mark of Divine Descent. This mark gives humans special abilities and defines their way of thinking and even your fighting style.
This is not a perfect world, but people don’t need to worry about discrimination depending on their mark, except in the city of Jericó.
