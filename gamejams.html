Returning Dream

GDSG IntrinsictGameJam 2020

Game made in a 4 members team for a GameJam within 102 hours under the theme "Other World" and "The game ends where it Starts".
Game Description:
Returning Dream is a game made for the Intrinsic Game Jam where a young girl has to go grab a medicinal herb for her grandma inside the forest. But she has to be caerful in the way back, as the forest has mysterious properties that turn everything into a dreamy-like world.

Cotxe11SummerParty

Gran CITM Game Jam

Game made in a 4 members team for a GameJam within 31 hours under the theme The Sea.
Game description:
This game is an endless runner, where you have to play for as much time as you can before losing the three lifes you are given. The player controls a car that goes through the sea and has to dodge the obstacles he encounters. Within the obstacles there are ones that take out one life and others that makes the game more difficult (limit vision, invert controls and extra speed) during a limited amount of time.

The Chat

Barcelona Games World Game Jam

Game made in a 4 members team for a GameJam within 30 hours under the theme happines.
Winner of the Accesit special price.
Game Description:
The Chat is a survival game where the player has to stay alive as much time as possible, avoiding the attack of the angry emojis. To do that the player will have to shoot messages to the angry emojis in order to transform them into happy emojis.
