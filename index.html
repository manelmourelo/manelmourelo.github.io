Game Design and Development

I’m Manel Mourelo, a videogame developer based in Barcelona.
I've loved videogames since I was a kid and I started developing games at age 15.
I started learning C, then I discovered Unity and felt in love with it, learning
C# and JavaScript along the way. When it was time to decide what I wanted
to do for living I decided to study Game Design and Development at CITM (UPC),
where I learned different programming languages and made different projects
such as making my own game engine and working in a VR project
in collaboration with Volkswagen.
