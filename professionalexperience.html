The Algian Sword

The Algian Sword

Zero Element Games

Programmer

The Algian Sword is a narrative rol videogame that takes place in a rich medieval fantasy universe, where the player can embody one of the three charismatic protagonists of the story: Ross, Nym and Jared. Through the decision making, the mysteries of the story will be revealed, and the traits of the characters will be modified, leading to different endings. The Algian Sword seeks to create a unique player experience through the mix of a realistic 3D aesthetic and pixelart characters and a combat system that combines elements of turn base and real time. At the moment, The Algian Sword is being developed with the pre-incubation program of Level Up (Game Dev Hub) in collaboration with Barcelona’s town hall.
